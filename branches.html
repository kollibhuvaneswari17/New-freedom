IBOGAINE IN THE ECOSYSTEM

Where Ibogaine Fits in the Model

Ibogaine isn’t a standalone solution. It sits inside a coordinated infrastructure that includes treatment, training, physical sites, and ongoing data from NF-167.

In Treatment

Medically Supervised Care

Within the treatment branch, ibogaine is framed as a medically supervised intervention derived from a serious therapeutic tradition and delivered in a controlled residential setting with screening, physician oversight, and follow-through around the procedure.

In Operations

Not A Casual Wellness Tool

Ibogaine requires careful operating discipline, including stabilization, medical screening, cardiac review, medication review, monitored care, and a trained team around the treatment window. That is why the surrounding infrastructure matters.

In Development

Future Therapeutic Development

Within the drug development branch, the focus expands into ibogaine pathways, noribogaine and receptor-system research, analog discovery, and future therapeutic programs designed to become safer, more scalable, and more clinically robust over time.
