HOW THE INFRASTRUCTURE OPERATES

New Freedom Life is designed to support the person in care, strengthen the care team, and connect the larger ecosystem around them.

FOR INDIVIDUALS IN CARE

The goal is not to replace treatment. It is to make the time between visits more stable by keeping the person connected to routines, guidance, and support.

1. Check-in

Small daily actions help track stress, mood, and recovery consistency

→

2. Get Guidance

The system can prompt grounding tools, routines, and next steps when risk starts to rise

→

3. Reach Support

When self-guided tools are not enough, the path back to help is meant to be quicker and clearer

FOR CLINICAL TEAMS

The aim is to give providers and operators more context between sessions, so care is not managed from a weekly snapshot alone.

1. Establish a baseline

Care starts with a clearer picture of the person’s condition, patterns, and needs.

→

2. Watch for change

Signals between visits can show when regulation is improving or when distress is rising.

→

3. Respond earlier

Earlier context can support faster outreach, better follow-through, and more consistent care planning.
