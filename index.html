CONNECTED INFRASTRUCTURE

New Freedom Life is not a single app or a single facility. It is an integrated recovery infrastructure model designed to connect people, care teams, and operating systems rather than leave them in separate silos.
At the center is NF-167, a digital operating layer designed to support the hours between formal sessions, when continuity of care is often weakest.
Around that layer, the ecosystem brings together treatment, training, physical infrastructure, and ibogaine-related drug development so the model can scale with greater continuity, clearer standards, and a longer-term clinical foundation.
